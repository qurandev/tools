Text Difference Visualizer - free tool for comparing text files and visualizing text differences       changed inserted deleted same
This freeware program can help you to visualize the differences between two texts. You can copy and paste the text into the text boxes, choose text files by selecting them, or just drag and drop the files from your desktop. (Your files will NOT be posted to a server, the program executes on the client)
